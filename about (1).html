About TISK English Medium School

TISK English Medium School, established in 1988 at Kovvappuram, Kannur District, Kerala, is a reputed CBSE-affiliated institution dedicated to delivering quality education from Grade I to Grade X.

With over 35 years of academic excellence, the school has consistently maintained 100% pass results in the CBSE Class X Board Examinations, with many students securing top marks and First Class distinctions.

Currently serving around 500 students, TISK provides a safe, disciplined, and inspiring learning environment supported by qualified and experienced teaching and non-teaching staff.

Our Vision

To create a nurturing and future-ready learning environment that empowers students to become critical thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens, inspired by innovation, integrity, and excellence.

Our Mission

  • To provide high-quality CBSE education for students from Grade I to Grade X.
  • To foster intellectual, emotional, social, and ethical development.
  • To encourage innovation, creativity, and critical thinking through experiential learning.
  • To promote inclusivity, respect, and diversity within a supportive school community.
  • To instill values of integrity, empathy, discipline, and responsible citizenship.
  • To prepare students to excel confidently in a rapidly evolving global world.

Principal's Message

Mr. Khasim P.K.
Principal, TISK English Medium School

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-Wishers,

It gives me immense pride and pleasure to welcome you to TISK English Medium School, Kovvappuram — an institution that has been shaping young minds with dedication and excellence since its establishment in 1988.

For more than three decades, TISK has stood as a beacon of quality education in Kannur district, committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Affiliated to CBSE, our school provides a strong academic foundation while nurturing creativity, character, and confidence among our students.

The academic year 2022–23 has been a year of joyful learning, vibrant celebrations, and remarkable achievements. Our students once again brought laurels to the institution by securing 100% pass results in the CBSE Class X Board Examination, with a significant number achieving First Class and top scores. Such consistent success reflects the dedication of our students, the commitment of our teachers, and the strong support of our parents.

Beyond academics, we believe education must prepare children for life. Through well-structured club activities, art festivals, Olympiads, exhibitions, sports meets, educational tours, and leadership opportunities like the School Parliament Election, we empower students to discover their talents and develop leadership qualities.

Our Science, Mathematics, Language, Social Science, and Mazhavil (Arabic) Clubs actively promote experiential learning. Initiatives such as Harithanganam (organic farming awareness), Anti-Drug Campaigns, Anti-Poverty Day activities, CPR awareness sessions, and visits to old age homes help instill values of compassion, social responsibility, and humanity in our children.

Infrastructure development has also been a priority. The inauguration of our new Administrative Block and the upgradation of our Computer Lab, Science Lab, and Library facilities reflect our commitment to providing a modern and resourceful learning environment.

At TISK, we focus on nurturing critical thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible citizens. Our aim is not only to help students score high marks but also to help them grow into confident individuals who can contribute positively to society.

As we move forward, we remain committed to excellence, innovation, and value-based education. Together, with the cooperation of parents, staff, and the community, we will continue to create an environment where every child can dream, explore, and succeed.

Let us continue this journey of learning and growth with confidence and determination.


Warm Regards,
Khasim P.K.
Principal
TISK English Medium School
Kovvappuram, Kannur, Kerala
